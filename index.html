A few months ahead for the upcoming prelims. Are you still confused to cover the full syllabus?
Don’t panic, don’t make yourself a pressure cooker. Mugging up entire study materials is not a good method to cover the syllabus.
For that, you need better understanding of UPSC, its exam pattern and the materials they have used to frame questions for the past decade.
So, the only solution to crack the prelims is decoding the syllabus, exam & question pattern but we know that in a short run you don’t have much time to analyze these.
